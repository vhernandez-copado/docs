## Overview

Agentia™ is Copado’s AI-native platform for managing AI agents across the Salesforce delivery lifecycle. It enables teams to build, deploy, govern, and improve AI agents directly within DevOps workflows.

Unlike traditional AI tools that operate in isolation, Agentia embeds agents into the delivery process and connects them to a shared context layer. This approach—AgentOps—ensures consistent, governed, and production-ready AI execution with human-in-the-loop control. 

## Why Does Agentia Exist?

Traditional AI tools, such as general-purpose chat assistants, operate outside the delivery workflow and produce isolated outputs. Because they lack a shared context layer, results vary across users and cannot be consistently governed.

This creates a fundamental tension: teams are under pressure to accelerate delivery, but cannot fully trust AI within production workflows. For Salesforce teams, this challenge is amplified by complex environments, strict governance requirements, and the need to coordinate across multiple orgs. They require a structured path from manual and assisted delivery to automated and ultimately autonomous delivery, without sacrificing control or quality. 

## How Does Agentia Work? 

Agentia is built on a human-in-the-loop operating model: humans define intent, agents execute tasks within guardrails, and humans verify outcomes. This ensures both speed and control across the delivery lifecycle.

Agentia provides a unified experience layer with guided workflows for structured tasks and an expert CLI for advanced operations.

Work begins in Agentia StudioTM, where you can define intent and manage projects. Agentia then applies context-aware intelligence, called Org Intelligence, from the Agentia AI Context HubTM—a centralized hub combining Salesforce metadata, organisational knowledge, and third-party integrations—to execute tasks across the delivery lifecycle.

Copado DevOps AI Agents operate across all stages of delivery: plan, build, test, release, and operate. Because all agents draw from the same shared context, outputs are consistent and aligned with organisational policies.

Agentia supports a progression from: 

1. Assisted delivery: agents support individual tasks. 
2. Automated delivery: agents execute workflows. 
3. Autonomous delivery: agents operate independently. 

This allows organisations to adopt AI at their own pace while maintaining governance. 

## Core Agentia Platform Products 

Agentia is composed of integrated products that work together across the delivery lifecycle. 

### Build and Design 

Agentia Studio  (Coming soon): An agent and workflow builder used to design agents, define workflows, configure agent behavior, and orchestrate automation. 

### Context and Org Intelligence 

Agentia AI Context Hub (formerly Copado AI Platform): A context and workspace management product that connects Salesforce metadata, knowledge documents, and third-party integrations to feed agents with Org Intelligence. 

### Delivery and Governance 

Agentia Pipeline<sup>TM</sup>: The DevOps foundation for managing environment deployments, release workflows, and governance controls. 

### Quality and Testing 

- Agentia Testing<sup>TM</sup> (formerly Copado Robotic Testing): A built-in validation product that ensures quality for prompts, agent responses, and metadata changes before release. Observability and Ecosystem 
- Agentia Mission Control<sup>TM</sup> (Coming soon): An observability product for monitoring agent performance, system health, and runtime diagnostics in production. 
- Agentia Marketplace<sup>TM </sup>(Coming soon): A hub for discovering and publishing reusable assets like prompts, templates, and MCP (Model Context Protocol) servers.
